Our story

First steps to Nepal were directed by completely different intention than it turned out later on. Data collection for my Master’s theses put me on to the exciting journey of exploring the superb tea and getting closer to a Nepali farmers community.

I have engaged in Nepal’s people everyday life in the breath-taking scenery of the Ilam reagion and admired by the taste of highland tea. However, on another side I sow the challenging conditions tea people face on daily basis, while trying to survive as tea workers and growers in Nepal. This story was a foundation to consider possible engagement to bring a positive change into the lives of these genuine people.
